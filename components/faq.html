Why should you choose an electronic record of working hours? Electronic records of working hours help to process working hours faster and more accurately in the company, thereby avoiding the paper form of managing working hours. You can access your time records anywhere and anytime. All data is stored in one place, and all data is also securely archived for possible viewing of past recorded working hours.
What does TimeKeeper offer? TimeKeeper is a comprehensive system for recording and managing the working hours of employees. The application offers the employee and supervisor a complete record of attendance and absence from the workplace. Recorded hours can be of different types, you can record arrivals / departures to the workplace, but also different types of absences - sick leave, holidays, special leave, study leave, travel assignments, etc.
How are working hours recorded? The entry of working hours into the working time record is done via mobile application. The employee or the administrator can take care of manual entry of working hours of different types. With a simple click, the user adds the working hours that were carried out today, and an unlimited number of working hours of different types can be entered on a single day.
How is the registration of working hours done? Registration of working hours is possible in several ways. One of the most common ways is to register working hours through the mobile app using a card or the key obtained by the employee. The mobile app can be installed at the entrance of the company and it ensures precise records.
